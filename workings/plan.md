## <u>Good Vibrations</u>
* Voice Recognition Game
* Dog Simulator

### World
  * Park
  * Trees, Grass, Bushes
  * Other Dogs

## Speech rules
###### 1. Start Menu
 * Start/Play/Okay/Continue - 'Game'
 * Exit/Quit


###### 2. Select Dog Menu - 6 Dogs
 * Next/No/Nah/Nope
 * Select/Yes/Yeah


###### 3. In-Game Menu
  * Pause - 'Game'
  * Resume - 'Game'
  * Main Menu
  * Exit/Quit


###### 4. Movement - Low Profile
  * Idle/Yield/Stop
  * Walk/Go


###### 5. Movement - High Profile
  * Run/Sprint
  * Jump/Hop
  * Bark


###### 6. Movement - Direction
  * Turn/Go - 'Right'
  * Turn/Go - 'Left'
